Assignment 4: Blobby the Blobfish :)

Instructions

Use the left and right arrow keys to move the blobfish left and right. Catch sea urchins to increase your score. If you crash into a jellyfish, you will lose a life, but if you click on a jellyfish you can gain a life. Use the spacebar to toggle between sad and happy modes. The game ends when you have lost all lives. Press the 'Restart' button below to restart the game.
